# Vendor Onboarding Process

_Document ID: POL-FIN-004_

## Required Documents
Before a new vendor can receive payment, the following must be submitted to Procurement:
1. Trade license copy
2. VAT registration certificate (if applicable)
3. Bank details with letterhead confirmation
4. Beneficial owner declaration
5. Signed Meridian Vendor Code of Conduct

## Due Diligence
Vendors with annual contract value over AED 100,000 are subject to enhanced due diligence including sanctions screening and adverse media checks.

## Onboarding Time
Standard vendor onboarding takes 5-10 working days. Urgent onboarding available with department head escalation.

## Payment Terms
Standard payment terms are 30 days net from invoice receipt. Payment terms cannot be shortened below 15 days without CFO approval.
